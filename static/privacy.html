We collect information you provide directly when you create an account or use the Service:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, mobile phone number, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Group & Game Data: Group memberships, game signups, waitlist positions, and scheduling preferences.
  • Communication Preferences: Your notification preferences (email, SMS, or none).

We automatically collect limited technical information:

  • Log Data: IP address (for security/rate-limiting only), login timestamps, and request metadata.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to operate and improve the Service:

  • Provide the Service: Manage your account, process game signups, run player selection algorithms, and organize groups.
  • Communicate with You: Send game notifications, signup confirmations, waitlist updates, and group invitations via your preferred channels (email and/or SMS).
  • Verify Your Identity: Send email verification codes to confirm your email address.
  • Security: Detect and prevent unauthorized access, rate-limit login attempts, and protect against abuse.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We will never do this.

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies:

  • Twilio: SMS delivery for game notifications (if you enable SMS).
  • Cloudflare: DNS, CDN, and DDoS protection.
  • DigitalOcean: Cloud hosting infrastructure.

We do not use any advertising networks, social media trackers, or behavioral analytics tools.

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained until you request deletion.
  • Game and signup history: Retained for the operation of the Service.
  • Notification logs: Retained for troubleshooting and may be periodically purged.
  • Login attempt records: Retained for security purposes and periodically purged.
  • Verification codes and password reset tokens: Expire within 24 hours and are marked as used.

7. Your Controls

You have direct control over your data through the app:

  • Leave a group: Every email includes a "Remove me from this group" link, or leave via Player Settings.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Authentication tokens expire and can be revoked.
  • The database uses WAL mode with foreign key enforcement.

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
